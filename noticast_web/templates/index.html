What is NotiCast?

NotiCast is a system that enables seamless text-to-speech notifications with no human interaction required. It can be useful for ongoing notifications without the hassle of having to communicate with the device.

With NotiCast, you can make interaction with notifications more seamless. Your phone doesn't make you click a notification to see the content, it just shows you it. You can hook up anything to NotiCast, such as custom monitoring software, incoming emails, and even a system to tell you a bad joke every 15 minutes.

Enabling your NotiCast Device

Skip these steps if you have your flash drive ready to go.

When you receive your NotiCast device, it should already have the software installed. If the device provider has not already provided you with a flash drive containing your authentication credentials, or if you lose this flash drive, you will need to set up a new drive. Assuming you have requested and received credentials from the provider, you can follow the steps below to set up a new flash drive.

  1. Plug in the flash drive and ensure that the filesystem is fat32
    • On Windows, you can reformat to change filesystem
    • On MacOS, you can use the "Disk Utility" program to erase and format
  2. Log in to the application and create a new device
    • You can skip this step if you just want to remake credentials
  3. Click the "Regen Credentials" button for the relevant device
  4. Copy the cert.crt and key files to the flash drive
  5. Plug the new flash drive into the device
  6. Boot (or reboot) your system and play a test message
